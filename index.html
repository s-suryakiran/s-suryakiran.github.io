Projects

DriveVLM

Developed a novel method to tackle supervised data scarcity in autonomous driving by fine-tuning the QwenVL Vision-Language Model. Tested on CARLA driving routes, our method processed visual inputs to generate precise vehicle control commands. With just 5% of the data, we achieved 40% of the traditional models' performance, closely matching them at 20%, and surpassing at 40%, showcasing the potential of Vision-Language Models in enhancing data efficiency.
